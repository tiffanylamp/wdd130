History

How It Started

White water rafting began as exploration, not recreation. In 1869, John Wesley Powell led one of the first recorded river expeditions through the Grand Canyon on the Colorado River. What started as a daring journey to map unknown waters eventually grew into a popular outdoor adventure as raft design and safety improved in the 20th century.

Our company continues that tradition of adventure and respect for the river. We combine modern safety practices with the same spirit of teamwork and courage that defined early river runners, creating exciting and memorable experiences on every trip.
